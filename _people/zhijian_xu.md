### Bio
I'm a PhD student in the Computer Science and Engineering Department at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. XU Qiang. Currently, I'm working on the time series problems in machine learning. Prior to this, I pursued my undergraduate degree in Zhejiang University. Back in ZJU, I joined the USSLAB and worked on the sensor security.


My research interests are now mainly about predicting and analyzing time series using machine learning methods. I'm also interested in the security problems in artificial intelligence and IoT.
