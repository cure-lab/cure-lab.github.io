### Bio
I’m a Research Assistant in the Computer Science and Engineering Department at the Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. XU Qiang. Currently, I’m working on gesture recognition problems in machine learning. Before this, I work as a research assistant at Stanley Ho Big Data Decision Analytics Research Centre in CUHK. I received my master's degree in Computer Science at CUHK in 2020. Before this, I received my bachelor degree at the Communication University of China.

My research interests are now mainly about analysing body gestures using machine learning methods. I’m also interested in problems in computer security.
