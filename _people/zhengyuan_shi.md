### Bio
Zhengyuan Shi is a first-year PhD student in CURE Lab. His research interests include Machine Learning in EDA, VLSI Computer-aid Design and Reconfigurable Computing. He received BEng in Information Engineering from Shandong University (SDU) in 2021 and was the recipient of SDU Presidential Scholarship, Baosteel Scholarship and SDU Outstanding Undergraduate Thesis Award. He was also a visiting student in University of British Columbia (Jul. 2018 - Aug. 2018) and worked as intern in Xilinx (Jul. 2019), 2012 Laboratory Huawei(Jun. 2020 - Oct. 2020) and Hisilicon Huawei(Feb. 2021 -).
