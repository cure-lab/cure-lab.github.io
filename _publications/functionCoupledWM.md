title: On Function-Coupled Watermarks for Deep Neural Networks 
description: To protect the IP of DNN models, we propose a novel DNN watermarking solution that can effectively defend against some prevailing attacks. Our key insight is to enhance the coupling of the watermark and model functionalities such that removing the watermark would inevitably degrade the model's performance on normal inputs.
